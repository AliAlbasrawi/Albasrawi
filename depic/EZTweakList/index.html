Easily export and share a list of all packages/tweaks on your device with EZTweakList! Tweak lists are essential for developers and users to help effectively troubleshoot bugs and issues that you encounter.

To use EZTweakList simply open up Cydia, tap on the "Installed" tab and then tap on the export/share button (located on the top navigation bar).

_________________________

No respring is required after installation, however Cydia will need to be restarted. There are no options to configure. Please contact me if you have any questions or need any additional information or support.
