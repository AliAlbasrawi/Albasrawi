Reveals HIDDEN cellular data usage details that Apple doesn't want you to see!
Compatible with iOS 8 ➜ 10.2
Did you know that the cellular data usage reported by iOS is inaccurate, and that Apple has been hiding some important details from you?


If you'd like to know the REAL story behind your mobile data usage then DetailedCellularUsage is just what you've been looking for!


Cellular data used by Apple's internal system apps and processes is hidden by default...but not for long once DCU is installed! In addition to fully revealing cellular data usage details, DCU also enables the ability to manage cell data access to select system apps, processes & daemons - giving you even more control over how your data is used.


DetailedCellularUsage is compatible with all cellular-capable iPhone's & iPad's running iOS 8 ➜ 10.2 (personally tested on iOS 8.4, 9.0.2, 9.3.3, 9.3.5, 10.1.1 & 10.2).


DCU is enabled by default after installation. You'll find a switch to enable/disable DCU on the default iOS Cellular Data page in the Settings app (ottom of the page). Enabling or disabling DCU will automatically trigger the cellular usage report to refresh.
