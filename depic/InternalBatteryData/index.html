Internal Battery Data
DEPICTION
iOS 9 - iOS 11.1.2 Compatible
Unlock Apple's hidden internal battery usage menu, along with it's features and data. More than just a working replacement for DetailedBatteryUsage on iOS 11, Internal Battery Data also adds new features - such as battery health and live usage stats. Discover the battery usage data that Apple doesn't want you to see! Enable/disable via Settings > Battery (enabled by default).
_______________________________

No respring is required after installation, however the Settings app will need to be restarted. Please contact me if you have any questions or need any additional information or support.

NOTE: Tap on Temperature to switch between Metric and Imperial units. Press and hold battery detials to copy to clipboard.
