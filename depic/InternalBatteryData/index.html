DetailedPowerUsage
iOS 9 - iOS 11.1.2 Compatible
Unlock Apple's hidden internal battery usage menu, along with its data and statistics. More than just a working replacement for DetailedBatteryUsage for iOS 11, DetailedPowerUsage also adds new features - such as battery health and live usage stats. Discover the battery usage data that Apple doesn't want you to see! Enable/disable via Settings > Battery (enabled by default).
_________________________

No respring is required after installation, however the Settings app will need to be restarted. Please contact me if you have any questions or need any additional information or support.

TIPS:

• Tap on temperature to switch between Metric and Imperial units

• Press and hold details to copy to clipboard

• View extended app power usage reports by pressing down and then sliding left on it’s table cell (similar to the “swipe to delete” motion)

• Live data updated every 20 seconds (this is because I use the existing power logs which iOS generates by default every 20 seconds…preventing DetailedPowerUsage from being a battery drain itself)
