Adorable Waddling Duck Animated Respring Theme

DEPICTION

  • Made as a request from r/jailbreak, this adorable duck walks with swagger while your device resprings. Requires the animated respring theming platform Springy. Check out the YouTube video below for a quick demo!


    Designed to fit the following devices: iPhone 5s/6/6+/6s/6s+/SE/7/7+


    Use the following settings in Springy: FPS = 16, Select "Loop Infinitely".
