  • What is NoFAMILYCIRCLED?

    NoFAMILYCIRCLED prevents (com.apple.familycircled) the FAMILYCIRCLED daemon from loading and running. Automatically launches even after reboot/restart. Helps reduce battery drain in certain circumastances. No settings to configure.

  • REBOOT REQUIRED AFTER INSTALLATION AND UNINSTALLATION. THIS INSTALLER WILL NOT AUTOMATICALLY REBOOT YOUR DEVICE! **NO REBOOT REQUIRED IF UPGRADING FROM AN OLDER VERSION OF NoFAMILYCIRCLED**
