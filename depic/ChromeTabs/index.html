ChromeTabs

  • Unlock the Google Chrome Tabbed Browsing Interface on iPhone/iPod!

    BETA VERSION


    ChromeTabs enables the Google Chrome iPad tabbed switcher interface on your iPhone/iPod!


    Supported Devices: All iPhone "Plus" models (6/6s/7). Non-Plus devices may experience bugs (listed below). Requires Chrome app version 56.0.2924.79 or newer.
