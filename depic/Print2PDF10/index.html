A VIRTUAL PDF Document Printer for iOS 10
DEPICTION
Compatible with iOS 10.1 ➜ 10.2.1
Print2PDF10 is a free PDF document generator for iOS 10 devices which adds a virtual "printer" to the default iOS printer menu.

Utilizing a special virtual printer driver that is recognized by iOS as a "regular" desktop printer, Print2PDF10 enables PDF document creation from almost any in-app content that you wish to preserve!

Print2PDF10 will appear as an available printer in the stock iOS print menu (accessable via in-app "Share" feature).

To print a virtual PDF document:
• Tap "Share" button from the app you wish to print from
• Choose the "Print" activity
• Select the "Print2PDF 10" printer from Printer Options
• Tap on "Print"
• A preview of your PDF will then appear, from which you can use the "Share" feature to save, copy, share or open the PDF file in an external app of your choosing.

Note: Hyperlinks are not retained once printed to a PDF document (this is due to iOS limitations).

There are no options to configure after installation. Special thanks to developer lordscotland for open sourcing his iOS 8 PDF printer tweak, from which Print2PDF10 shares base code!
