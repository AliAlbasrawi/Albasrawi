Don't Touch That Dial!

DEPICTION

  • Stop notification sounds from interrupting your music!

    BETA Software | iOS 8-10.x


    Have you ever been jamming out to a favorite song only to have the beat drop when you get a text? Or maybe you replied to a message while listening to music and were welcomed with an ear full of keyboard clicks? These scenerios - and many more - are a thing of the past with Don't Touch That Dial!


    This version (1.x) of DTTD doesn't depend on mobilesubstrate, which means it persists in Safe Mode as well as in un-jailbroken state. DTTD v2.0 (which is almost ready for BETA release) does depend on mobilesubstrate, so it will only work while jailbroken and not in Safe Mode.


    There are no options to configure...just install and you're ready to go!
