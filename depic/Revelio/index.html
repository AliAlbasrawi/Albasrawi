Revelio, named after the revealing spell from Harry Potter, is a customizable fade-in animation tweak which makes your homescreen app icons appear after a defined time.

This tweak is the polar opposite of "Evanesco"...a tweak from CPDigitalDarkroom which makes your homescreen app icons disappear after a set amount of time.
_______________________________
Available animation triggers include:

● Fade-in from unlock
● Fade-in from home button press
● Fade-in after exiting folders

Animation speed is configurable from 1 second up to 15 seconds for each trigger. Triggers can also be disabled individually.
_______________________________
Revelio is compatible with all iOS devices running iOS 10.x through 10.2.1. Tested on the following versions:

iOS 10.1.1 & 10.2
All options are enabled by default, so please visit the Settings app to configure if desired. Respring required after installation...but not usually necessary when changing settings. Respring button provided in Settings for convenience.
