Extended Clips

  • Install Apple's new Clips 1.0 app on iOS 10.1+


    Enables installation of the Clips app on iOS 10.1 - 10.2, bypassing Apple's artificial iOS 10.3 requirement. Supports 64-bit devices on iOS 10.1 and above due to required frameworks.



    After installation navigate to /var/mobile/Documents/ExtendedClips where you'll find 2 directories: Compile Your Own and Compiled IPA Example. My suggestion is to compile your own version of Clips using the modified files located in Compile Your Own, however I've provided an already compiled version for "example" use located in Compiled IPA Example directory.
