FolderCloserXI
Compatible with iOS 8, 9, 10 & 11!
Irritated that folders stay open after exiting apps? Avoid the “folder trap” and improve usability with FolderCloserXI.

After launching an app from a folder, FolderCloserXI automatically closes it for you; then when you exit the app you will be on your home screen instead of the stuck inside the folder!

FolderCloserXI is compatible with all jailbroken devices running iOS 8.x thru 11.1.2.

What's New in v1.0.2-1?
Added iOS 8, 9 & 10 compatibility!
Respring required after installation. FolderCloserXI is enabled by default, and can be disabled/enabled via the Settings app.
